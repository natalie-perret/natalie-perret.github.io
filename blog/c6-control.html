Cultural blog 7

Control

When programming, I often get a feeling of helplessness from situations where I lack control. This often triggers a disproportionate emotional response.

In Search Inside Yourself, Chade-Meng Tan introduces the practice called ",SiBerian North RailRoad" for dealing with triggers. The practice has five steps: Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, and Respond. This is an important exercise to deal with negative and distressing emotions.
