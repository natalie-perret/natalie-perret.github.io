Kia ora. Hello. Hola. 你好. Bonjour. Привет. こんにちは. Kamusta. Guten Tag.

Hi, I'm Natalie and I was born in New Zealand to Swiss parents. I grew up speaking English and French and I've spent most of my life learning different languages and travelling the world. I've attempted to learn seven languages and consider myself fluent in English and French, and can get by in Spanish and Chinese (Mandarin). I've travelled to over 45 countries and lived and worked in five of them. And if my visa application gets accepted, number six will be Canada.

So why code? Well I've been told that code is like a language so it was inevitable that I'd end up there eventually and I'm hoping that it will open up lots of new opportunities for me to travel the world and make a difference.
